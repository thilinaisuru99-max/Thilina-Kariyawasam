Studio Note

Every room begins as a question.

How does the light fall at four in the afternoon. How does a countertop age after a decade of use. How does a family actually move through a kitchen, or a team through an office floor. The work collected here — kitchens, bedrooms, living spaces, a residential façade, a workplace — is where those questions get tested, room by room, before they're built.

Renders are treated as working drawings, not decoration: a way to check proportion, material, and light against reality ahead of time.

About

Thilina Isuru Kariyawasam is an interior designer and interior architect based in Sri Lanka, working across residential and hospitality interiors. Each project moves from concept sketch to fully considered 3D visualization — material, light, and layout resolved before construction begins.

His practice runs alongside ongoing research into sustainable, healthy interior materials — an interest that quietly shapes how finishes are specified, how rooms are ventilated, and how each space is detailed.
